History of Niğde Fen Lisesi

Niğde Fen Lisesi is a high school in Niğde/Merkez. It is the educational institution that started its educational activities in the 1999-2000 academic year.Since it was found, it has been one of the first choices of students not only in the city where it is located, but also in the surrounding provinces. In the 2000-2001 academic year, it moved to its own building and continues its educational activities. It was moved from its old building in the 2019-2020 academic year.
